United Republic of Tanzania

United Republic of Tanzania is made up of two main parts, which are Tanganyika, the mainland and Zanzibar, a combination of two islands which are Pemba and Unguja. Tanganyika and Zanzibar were merged in 1964 to form Tanzania. The decision to unite the two came from the leader of Tanganyika, the late Mwalimu Julius K. Nyerere and from the leader of Zanzibar, the late Abeid A. Karume. Tanzania is known woldwide as a place rich in game reserves and national parks.

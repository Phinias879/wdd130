History

Mountain View Rafting Company was incorporated in 1991 at Mississippi. The company started with a small number of experts dedicated to bring satisfaction to clients. In 2005, the company expanded its operations, covering other areas, providing high quality service. To date the company has over 45 offices in USA only, and 200 in other parts of the world. From the time the company started up to now, it has earned more than 50 awards in customer service from different institutions, and has been nominated more than 60 times to be the best rafting company in USA. Truly, we can say that we are the best when it comes to adventures in rivers.
